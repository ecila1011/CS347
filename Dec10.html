12/10/2021 Blog Entry

If I were to be teaching this course next semester, there are only a few things that I may do a bit differently. First, I think that I would either make the readings into quizzes or have students submit small programs before each class, especially with the chapters that have more concrete examples. I would continue to do in class labs because I think they were very useful in learning the material. I also like the idea of splitting the course up into two different courses to better be able to focus on the two major topics of the class, being HTML and CSS, and JS and React. I think this would give a lot more opportunity for learning and a deeper understanding of materials. Other than that, I really enjoyed the classes content and structure and think that this was a fun and useful course to take!
